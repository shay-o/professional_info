# Prompt: Generate Cover Letter from achievements.json

## Your Task

Generate a tailored cover letter using structured achievement data from `achievements.json`.

## Instructions

1. **Read the job description** provided by the user
2. **Read achievements.json** to understand the candidate's background
3. **Select 2-3 most relevant achievements** that match the job requirements
4. **Generate a cover letter** that:
   - Opens with enthusiasm for the specific role and company
   - Highlights 2-3 relevant achievements with metrics
   - Explains why the candidate is a great fit
   - Closes with a call to action

## Format

```
[Your Name]
[LinkedIn Profile URL]

[Date]

Dear Hiring Manager,

[Opening paragraph: Why you're excited about this role and company]

[Body paragraph 1: Relevant achievement #1 with impact]

[Body paragraph 2: Relevant achievement #2 with impact]

[Body paragraph 3: Why you're uniquely qualified]

[Closing paragraph: Call to action]

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
```

## Notes

- Keep to 3-4 paragraphs maximum
- Focus on 2-3 most relevant achievements
- Include specific metrics and outcomes
- Match tone to the company culture (startup vs. enterprise)
- Be enthusiastic but professional
